* Then they assign **Dr. Silva (UoP IT Coordinator)** as the **Category Manager**.
* Now, Dr. Silva can:

  * Set up conference details
  * Enable abstract submissions, registration, and room bookings
  * Assign further roles (e.g., editors, reviewers)
